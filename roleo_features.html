How It Works

Roleo is a modern, Tinder-style job discovery feature that lets users swipe through personalized job opportunities with ease. It automatically updates and aggregates listings from top job boards like Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, and ZipRecruiter, ensuring that the most recent and relevant positions are always shown. Users create a quick profile highlighting their skills, experience, and career preferences, and Roleo's smart matching algorithm then curates job cards that best fit their profile. Each card displays key details such as the company name, job title, salary range, work mode, and a short description. Users can swipe right to save or apply later, swipe left to skip, or tap the card to view full details and apply directly using pre-filled profile information. As users continue to swipe, Roleo's AI learns from their preferences and improves future recommendations. The platform includes a Liked Jobs dashboard for tracking saved opportunities, real-time alerts for expiring or similar roles, and additional features such as company insights, commute visualization, and anonymous browsing. With a fun, gamified interface and smart automation, Roleo transforms job hunting into a fast, efficient, and engaging experience, helping users find the perfect role with just a few swipes.
